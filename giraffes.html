Why Giraffes are so Awesome

Necks

The most notable feature of a giraffe is its exagerrated neck. Baby giraffes are bon six feet tall! This physical phenomena places them as the tallest species in the animal kingdom It comprises of a majority of their height. This beneficial attribute helps them ingest leaves from the top of trees. It also helps them look out for potential predators.In cases of potential danger, the giraffe uses it's powerful neck as a weapon. It necks with aforce of fifty to 60 Newtons. This is astounding compared to the limits of other animals.

Other Physical Features

Giraffes are coated with a skin pattern that is unique from other animals. It is yellow and covered with beautiful brown patches. Their tongues are two meters long and they weigh upto 1, 900 kgs.

Contribution to the Enviroment

Due to their ability to see threats from afar, giraffes also aid other animals evade possible danger. They are the first to run while the predators are still far. The other animals see this as a signal and escape.
